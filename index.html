Why Erin Mills Optical

The independent difference

  • Personal fittings, every time. The same experienced hands measure, fit and adjust your eyewear — no rotating cast, no rush.
  • Honest advice. We're not on commission to push this month's promotion. If a $150 frame suits you better than a $400 one, we'll say so.
  • Free lifetime adjustments. Glasses drift out of alignment; yours shouldn't. Drop in any time for a re-fit, on the house.
  • A neighbourhood fixture. We've been fitting Erin Mills families from the same plaza for years — and our customers keep coming back.

Direct insurance billing

Bring your benefits card and we'll bill your insurer directly where plans allow — including Sun Life — so you pay only the difference, not the wait. Ask us about your plan when you book.
